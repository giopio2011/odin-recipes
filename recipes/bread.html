BREAD

dicepsation:

A nice bread with a hard outside. Takes 1 1/2 hours to 2 hours.

Ingredints:

  • 1 pound flour
  • 2 tps salt
  • 2 tps yeast
  • 12 oz warm water

Steps:

  1. mix the flour and the salt in a middam sized bowl
  2. stir the yeast in the water
  3. let it set for 10 mintuts
  4. pour the water in to the flour and stir
  5. nead for 10 mintuts
  6. rest until doubled
  7. shape and put on pan
  8. preheat to 425
  9. rest until the oven is hot
  10. cook for 10 mintuts
